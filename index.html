GRAPHIC DESIGN

Toji Fushiguro × Under Armour — Concept Collaboration

I started this project with the idea of creating a series of character collaborations with brands that would naturally align with the character. The concept features Toji Fushiguro from Jujutsu Kaisen paired with Under Armour.

Toji’s intense physical ability and performance-driven personality made him an ideal choice to showcase Under Armour’s compression gear.

This concept emphasizes on strength, motion, and high-performance wear.

Disclaimer: This is an unofficial and non-commercial concept. I am not affiliated with Under Armour or the creator of Jujutsu Kaisen (Gege Akutami).

Tsukishima Kei × ASICS — Concept Collaboration

This concept features Tsukishima Kei from the series Haikyuu!! partnered with Asics, a global leader in volleyball footwear.

Asics appears frequently appears in the series under the fictional brand ‘ICS’, a play on the brands name. This reference reflects how the brand has influenced the volleyball industry, which only feels natural to create a collaboration between Haikyuu!! and Asics.

Tsukishima was chosen as the featured character due to his strategic approach to volleyball and his well known blocking ability in the series.

The goal of this concept was to highlight Asic’s core brand values - discipline and precision, which mirrors their ethos: “ Sound Mind, Sound Body ”.

Disclaimer: This is an unofficial and non-commercial concept.

GAME DESIGN

First-Person Shooter Level Design — University Project

This project focuses on level design for a first-person shooter prototype developed in Unreal Engine. The level was built through an iterative workflow, starting with a blockout mock-up to test scale, player flow, sightlines, and pacing before moving into a more refined layout.

Gameplay testing informed multiple revisions to improve navigation clarity, combat encounters, and overall player experience.
